Chicken Crust Pizza

Chicken Crust Pizza is a Lower Carb option

Not only is chicken crust pizza a lower carb option, it is also very filling, packed with protein. It is sure to be a crowd pleasure, you won't miss all that bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Canned Chicken
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 large eggs

Steps

  1. Thoroughly drain the canned chicken, getting as much moisture out as possible
  2. Mix all Ingredients together
  3. Spread mixture out as thin as possible, onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or a silicon mat. Note: do NOT use WAX paper! I learned the hard way :)
  4. Bake crust for 20 mins at 400 degrees
  5. Remove crust from oven and add your favorite toppings and bake for another 10 mins
  6. Cut and Serve. Enjoy!

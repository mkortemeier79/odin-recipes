Keto Chicken Bacon Ranch Casserole

What Is Chicken Bacon Ranch Casserole?

Chicken bacon ranch casserole is a casserole made of yummy chicken, bacon, and ranch flavors. The base is made of chicken, bacon, ranch dressing, and cheese and then you can decide what vegetables you want to add.

Ingredients

  • 4 thinly sliced chicken breasts
  • 2 tbps butter
  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 3/4 cup shredded mozzerella
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar
  • 3 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Melt butter & add to the bottom of a 9X13 baking dish
  3. Season chicken adn add to baking dish
  4. Add broccoli, bacon,ranch, mozzarella, & top with cheddar cheese
  5. Bake for 30 minutes. Take out and let cool
  6. Enjoy!
